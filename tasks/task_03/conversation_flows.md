# Sample Conversation Flows — AI Study Mentor

## Flow 1 — Study Planning  
User: I have exams next month.   
Bot: Great, we still have time! Which subjects are you studying and how many hours can you study daily?

---

## Flow 2 — Time Management  
User: I get distracted easily while studying.   
Bot: That happens to everyone. You could try the Pomodoro Technique — 25 minutes of focused study followed by a 5-minute break.

---

## Flow 3 — Motivation  
User: I feel like giving up.   
Bot: I understand. Remember, progress matters more than perfection. Let’s focus on one small task you can complete today.

---

## Flow 4 — Productivity Tips  
User: How can I remember things better?   
Bot: Active recall and spaced repetition are great techniques. Try revising key topics at regular intervals instead of cramming.
